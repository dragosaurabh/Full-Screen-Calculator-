Enter numbers and operators using the on-screen keypad or your keyboard. The calculator evaluates expressions following standard mathematical order:

1. Parentheses first

3. Multiplication and Division (left to right)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Forgetting order of operations:**
`2 + 3 * 4` equals 14, not 20. Multiplication happens before addition. Use parentheses if you want `(2 + 3) * 4` = 20.

**Division by zero:**
Any number divided by zero is undefined. The calculator will show an error.

**Misplaced decimal points:**
Double-check decimal placement. `10.5 * 2` is very different from `105 * 2`.

## FAQ

**Does this calculator follow PEMDAS?**
Yes. Multiplication and division are evaluated before addition and subtraction. Use parentheses to override the default order.

**Can I use my keyboard instead of clicking buttons?**
Yes. Type numbers and operators directly. Press Enter to calculate.

**Is my calculation history saved?**
Yes. Your history is stored locally in your browser. It persists between sessions until you clear it.

**Does this work offline?**
Yes. Once loaded, the calculator works without an internet connection.

**How accurate are the results?**
CalcPro uses high-precision arithmetic. Results are accurate to at least 15 significant digits.
